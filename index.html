Bulls and Cows

The aim is to decipher the secret code by trial and error. The digits must be all different.If the matching digits are in their right positions, they are "bulls", if in different positions, they are "cows". Example: Secret number: 4271 Opponent's try: 1234 Answer: 1 bull and 2 cows. (The bull is "2", the cows are "4" and "1".)"
