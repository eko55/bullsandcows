Bulls and Cows

The aim is to guess the secret code with the lest attempts possible. The digits must be all different.If the matching digits are in their right positions, they are "bulls", if in different positions, they are "cows". Example: Secret number: 427 Opponent's try: 324 Answer: 1 bull and 1 cows. (The bull is "2", the cow is "4".)
